Privacy Policy for Kids Memory Card Game

Last updated:30 August 2025

1. Overview

We value your privacy. This app is designed for children under 13, and we comply with Google Play Families Policy requirements.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal or sensitive information from children under 13. We do not use tracking SDKs, device identifiers, or third-party analytics.

3. Use of Data

No personal data is collected or used. Our app does not show personalized ads. If ads are present, they come only from Google Play Families Self-Certified Ads SDKs.

4. Parental Consent & Access

If personal data is ever collected in the future, we will obtain verifiable parental consent before collection and provide a way for parents to review and delete their child’s data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not collect data, nothing is stored or retained. If data is collected in the future, it will only be kept as long as necessary and can be deleted upon request.

6. Data Security

We do not store any personal data. If collection occurs in the future, appropriate security measures will be implemented.

7. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. If third-party SDKs or APIs are used in the future, they will be disclosed here and will comply with children’s privacy standards.
